The Poor in America

In 100 years, America has continuously failed the poor. The unchanged state of poverty from 1920's to today has left generations of Americans stuck in a devastating cycle of poverty, a struggle that has been ignored by both the government and society.

This website is structured into the 4 ways that America supresses the poor. The two pages on the left are related to the the dynamics between the government and the poor, and the pages on the right relate to societal pressures on the poor. Each page describes the developments between the specific entity and the poor and how their relationships have worsened over the years. On each page, we have a short skit that shows short scenes of how the poor had to struggle in the 1920s and how that has not changed in today's world. The page is formatted like an "article" with supplemental graphs and data.
